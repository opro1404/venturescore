FAQ

Questions answered.

Everything you'd want to know before handing your idea over to an AI.

How is this different from ChatGPT?▾
ChatGPT will validate almost any idea you throw at it — it's designed to be helpful and agreeable. VentureScore is specifically prompted to be a brutally honest VC analyst. It produces a structured, scored report with consistent sections every time, not a conversational response. The format, depth, and specificity are incomparable.
How accurate is the analysis?▾
VentureScore is a powerful thinking tool, not a crystal ball. It applies established startup frameworks — market sizing, defensibility, competition, PMF — to your idea at AI speed. It will surface risks and opportunities you hadn't considered. Whether you act on them is still your call. Use it to stress-test your thinking, not to replace it.
Is my idea kept private?▾
Your idea is sent to our specialised AI to generate the report and is not stored or logged by VentureScore. We recommend not sharing anything genuinely trade-secret level in any AI tool.
Can I run multiple ideas?▾
Yes — the Monthly and Lifetime plans include unlimited analyses. The one-time plan covers a single report. Many users run 5–10 ideas in a session to quickly filter down to the strongest candidate before investing any time building.
What if my idea gets a low score?▾
That's actually the most valuable outcome. A low survival score with clear explanations tells you exactly what's broken and why — giving you the chance to pivot, sharpen, or kill the idea before you waste real money. A false-positive from a nicer tool is far more dangerous.
